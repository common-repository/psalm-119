Add an encouraging exerpt from Psalm 119 to the admin section of your Wordpress. 

== Description ==

This plugin adds an exerpt from Psalm 119 to the upper content area of the admin section of Wordpress and is based on Matt Mullenweg's 'Hello Dolly' plugin.

Example of the exerpts included: 

* Open my eyes that I may see
* Blessed are those who keep His statutes
* I am a stranger on earth
* Your statutes are my delight
* I seek You with all my heart
* Do not let me stray from Your commands
* I have hidden Your word in my heart
* Preserve my life according to Your word
* My soul is weary with sorrow; strengthen me according to Your word
* Give me understanding, so that I may keep Your law
* Turn my eyes away from worthless things
* Preserve my life according to Your word
* May Your unfailing love come to me, Lord
* I will always obey Your law
* I will speak of Your statutes before kings
* Remember Your word to Your servant
* Your promise preserves my life
* more...
		

== Installation ==

Install the plugin through the Plugins screen. No configuration neccissary.

e.g.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What version of the bible is used? =

The New International Version

= Can I control the order of the exerpts? =

No. This plugin was intended to present a simple random exerpt from Psalm 119 for encouragement. There is no configuration available.
